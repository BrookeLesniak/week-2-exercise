About Rose & Reign

Timeless elegance meets modern expertise — expert care for your crowning glory.

Our Story

Rose & Reign is a sophisticated yet approachable hair salon that combines timeless elegance with modern styling expertise. The name evokes natural beauty (Rose) and empowerment (Reign), positioning the salon as a place where clients discover their most confident, radiant selves.

Founded in 2018, what started as a two-chair studio has blossomed into a full-service luxury salon. At Rose & Reign, we believe your hair should make you feel like royalty every single day. Our expert stylists take the time to understand your unique beauty, lifestyle, and goals — creating looks that bloom with you, not against you.

  • Excellence — Commitment to superior skill and service
  • Authenticity — Real relationships, honest recommendations
  • Beauty — Celebrating diverse definitions of beautiful
  • Empowerment — Helping clients feel confident
  • Growth — Continuous learning and improvement
  • Community — Supporting clients and local connections
